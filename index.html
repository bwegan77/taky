Our Story

Bringing the Taste of Baja to San Diego

Founded in 2015, Taqueria Sol y Mar started as a small food truck near the beach. Our mission was simple: serve the most authentic Baja-style tacos using locally sourced ingredients and family recipes passed down through generations.

From our famous hand-battered fish tacos to our slow-cooked carne asada, everything is made fresh daily. We believe that great food brings people together, and we invite you to join our family table.
